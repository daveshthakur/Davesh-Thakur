Citizens Bank and Citizens One Home Loans

The Home Lending Solutions division operates under the Citizens Bank brand within our banking footprint: Massachusetts, Rhode Island, Connecticut, New Hampshire, Vermont, New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Delaware, Ohio and Michigan. Outside of this footprint, we market our home lending products under the Citizens One Home Loans brand and actively recruit loan officers in additional states.
